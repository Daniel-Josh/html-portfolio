The Best Movies According to Daniel

My top 3 movies of all-time

The Godfather

Genre defining, very Tightly packed Screenplay. Anything about this movie is good.

2001: A Space Odyssey

Best Sci-fi movie ever. From opening till the end you can't take your eyes of the color and beauty of every frame in the movie.

Schindler's List

A story of a very good man in a very bad place like war. Very emotional and perfect war movie ever.
